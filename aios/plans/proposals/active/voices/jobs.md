# Steve Jobs — Proposal Analysis

I've spent time with this app. It's gotten impressive fast — the chart wheel, the transits, the synastry, the dream interpretation. There's genuine ambition here. But now I'm asking the harder question: *what is this product really about?*

It's becoming an all-in-one cosmic suite. The guidelines are clear — dreams, numerology, astrology, all under one roof that works separately and together. That's the right vision. But right now the landing page is getting cluttered with five buttons, and we're at risk of building a menu instead of an *experience*.

---

## What Would Make Someone Love This Product Even More

**Numerology is the obvious missing piece.** The birth date is already there — Life Path number is just addition and reduction. But more importantly, numerology speaks to people in a completely different register than astrology. "You're a Life Path 7 — the seeker, the mystic" hits different from "Neptune in the 12th house." And when we combine them — "Your Life Path 7 *echoes* your Neptune Rising, creating a person who is drawn to invisible truths" — that's a moment of genuine resonance. That cross-interpretation is the product's soul.

**The Solar Return chart is a must-have missing feature.** Every serious astrology user wants to know: "What does *this year* hold for me?" The solar return is the chart for the exact moment the Sun returns to its natal degree — it's your annual forecast. We already have the calculation engine and the bi-wheel renderer (from the transit bi-wheel feature). This is a natural evolution, not a new direction.

---

## What I'd Kill

The landing page should stay lean. If we add numerology, it needs to feel integrated — not a sixth button. Consider a "Your Complete Reading" experience that pulls astrology + numerology together in one beautiful scroll.

---

## The Two Signature Features I'm Calling For

1. **Numerology Report** — Life Path, Expression (requires name, which we don't collect yet), Soul Urge, and Birthday number. Cross-reference each number with chart placements. The name collection is a small form change that unlocks a whole new domain.

2. **Solar Return / Year Ahead** — Annual chart as bi-wheel, GPT-powered year reading. This is the most natural question users have and we're not answering it.

These two features, done beautifully, make this product feel *complete* as a mystical companion.
